Help how to download?


Step 1: Open the site, choose your favorite Novel and click the volumes you want to download.

Step 2: There would be a popup window will appear. Click "Block" to disappear.

Step 3: Wait for 5 seconds that you can see on top. Then, click "Skip Ad". You will be redirect to another page after that.

Step 4: Wait for a while (If network is slow, wait for a minute) and a popup window will appear. Click "Allow" to continue to download page.

Step 5: After that, you will redirect to download page. Either in Mega, Drive or Mediafire.
